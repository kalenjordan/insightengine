Remember the last time your email system failed?

Maybe it was the cron that sends out reminder emails for your app, or maybe it was the code that delivers order confirmation emails for your eCommerce store.

You didn't even realize that email had stopped going out for like 4 days, until you got a call from an angry customer wondering why their shipping confirmation email was taking so long to get to them.

With InsightEngine, you'll be able to rest easy knowing that your Mandrill account is being proactively monitored. InsightEngine analyzes the sending patterns of your emails by tag, and if it notices a blip in sending activity, it notifies you.

Handy, right?

Step 1: Connect Mandrill

To get started, you just need to drop your Mandrill API key in. Then we'll grab your Mandrill tags and get to work.

Step 2: Manage Tags

Take a peek at your active Mandrill tags and feel free to toggle off any that don't need to be monitored.

Step 3: Get Notified

If there are any issues with any of your Mandrill tags, you'll get a notification email, so that you can dig in and figure it out.
